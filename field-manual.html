01
THE SNACK IS SACRED
Primary Mandate · All Operatives
COMPLETE

All snacks within a designated Wraff Force perimeter are considered mission-critical assets. They are to be treated with the same level of seriousness as any tactical objective. No snack is to be dismissed as trivial, regardless of size, flavour or caloric content.

An unsecured snack is a compromised snack. A compromised snack is a failed mission. A failed mission is documentation. All documentation is filed. All filed documentation is reviewed. The review takes time. Time is operational cost. The snack was not worth it.

📋 This regulation has never been violated without a subsequent incident report. The two events are considered connected. This has not been formally stated. It is understood.
02
73% IS THE BENCHMARK
Recovery Standards · Snack Ops Division
COMPLETE

The official Wraff Force snack recovery benchmark is 73%. This figure was established following the first operational assessment and has not been revised. It will not be revised. 73% represents acceptable operational performance. Above 73% is commendable. Below 73% requires documentation.

The benchmark of 73% applies across all mission types, all operatives and all snack categories. There are no exceptions. There are no adjustments for seasonal snacks, event-specific treats or chocolate reserves. 73% is 73%.

INCIDENTS ARE FILED
Documentation Protocol · All Operatives
COMPLETE

All incidents, regardless of severity, classification, or plausibility of the attached explanation, are to be formally filed. There is no minimum threshold for filing. A suspicion is sufficient. A feeling is sufficient. A smell is sufficient. Lt. Sniff has used all three as grounds for filing.

PERIMETER IS MAINTAINED
Field Operations · Perimeter Division
COMPLETE

A Wraff Force perimeter is a commitment. Once established, it is to be maintained until formally stood down by the primary operative. No operative may abandon their assigned perimeter position without authorisation. Exceptions are documented in Appendix A.
