AGENCY HISTORY
OVERVIEW

WRAFF FORCE was established as a fictional K-9 task agency to address a growing gap in global snack security and morale operations. Early field tests showed a significant correlation between stable treat supply and mission success – as well as tail-wag frequency.

Over time, WRAFF FORCE expanded from living-room perimeter patrols to full hallway coverage, couch-border control and high-priority cuddle zones. Today, the agency operates wherever snacks are stored under suspiciously flimsy protection.

THREAT LEVELS

The Treat Alert System indicates the current strategic relevance of snacks within the operational area.

LEVEL 0 – DORMANT CRUMBS
No active treats detected. Routine patrols only.
LEVEL 1 – VISIBLE SNACK ACTIVITY
Open snack packages observed in human custody. Increased vigilance recommended.
LEVEL 2 – UNATTENDED TREAT SCENARIO
Snacks temporarily left without direct supervision. High temptation, strict protocol required.
LEVEL 3 – FLOOR-LEVEL IMPACT EVENT
Treat has reached ground level. Immediate response authorized under sit–stay conditions.
LEVEL 4 – MULTI-TREAT SPILL
Multiple units scattered. Coordinated retrieval operations in effect.
LEVEL 5 – FULL BAG COMPROMISE
Entire treat container breached. All units on deck. Self-control at maximum priority.
HQ REGULATIONS
INTERNAL PROTOCOLS
  • Regulation 01 – Corridor Conduct
    Uncontrolled zoomies are restricted to designated zoomie lanes. Collisions with humans carrying hot beverages are to be avoided at all costs.
  • Regulation 02 – Treat Handling Protocol
    No operative may self-approve treat access. All snacks require human authorization, even if clearly mislaid on the coffee table.
  • Regulation 03 – Vocalization Policy
    Barking is permitted for security-relevant events only: doorbells, suspicious noises and the sound of treat bags. Excessive commentary on passing leaves or imaginary threats is discouraged.
  • Regulation 04 – Couch Occupation Rights
    Shared couch usage must follow the “One Paw Width” rule. Gradual expansion into human territory is allowed if no objection is raised.
  • Regulation 05 – Cuddle Priority
    During low-alert phases, cuddle requests from trusted humans have priority status. Operations may be temporarily paused for belly-rub objectives.

HQ-REGULARIEN
INTERNES PROTOKOLL
  • Regel 01 – Flurverhalten
    Unkontrollierte Zoomies sind auf ausgewiesene Zoomie-Spuren beschränkt. Kollisionen mit Menschen, die heiße Getränke tragen, sind unter allen Umständen zu vermeiden.
  • Regel 02 – Treat-Handling-Protokoll
    Kein Operative darf Treat-Zugriff eigenständig freigeben. Alle Snacks erfordern eine menschliche Autorisierung – selbst wenn sie offensichtlich unbeaufsichtigt auf dem Couchtisch liegen.
  • Regel 03 – Vokalisierungsrichtlinie
    Bellen ist nur bei sicherheitsrelevanten Ereignissen zugelassen: Türklingeln, verdächtige Geräusche und das Rascheln von Treat-Tüten. Übermäßiger Kommentar zu vorbeifliegenden Blättern oder imaginären Bedrohungen ist unerwünscht.
  • Regel 04 – Sofa-Belegungsrecht
    Gemeinsame Sofanutzung erfolgt nach der „Eine-Pfote-Abstand“-Regel. Eine schrittweise Expansion in den menschlichen Bereich ist zulässig, sofern kein Widerspruch erfolgt.
  • Regel 05 – Cuddle-Priorität
    In Phasen niedriger Alarmstufe haben Kuschelanfragen vertrauenswürdiger Menschen Priorität. Laufende Operationen dürfen zugunsten von Bauchrubbel-Zielen kurzfristig unterbrochen werden.
