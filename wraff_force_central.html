AGENCY HISTORY
OVERVIEW

WRAFF FORCE was established as a fictional K-9 task agency to address a growing gap in global snack security and morale operations. Early field tests showed a significant correlation between stable treat supply and mission success – as well as tail-wag frequency.

Over time, WRAFF FORCE expanded from living-room perimeter patrols to full hallway coverage, couch-border control and high-priority cuddle zones. Today, the agency operates wherever snacks are stored under suspiciously flimsy protection.

STRUCTURE & PERSONNEL
DIVISIONS · OPERATIVES
Operational Divisions
  • Recon Unit – “Eyes on the Floor” Silent hallway patrols, couch-edge surveillance and early detection of falling crumbs.
  • Snack Ops – “Treat Response Team” Rapid deployment to unsecured food sources, counter-snack operations and controlled sit–stay maneuvers.
  • Treat Logistics – “Supply & Distribution” Handling, transport and fair allocation of treats. Monitors stock levels and investigates mysteriously empty packages.
  • Cuddle Command – “Morale Core” Responsible for tactical cuddling, emotional de-escalation and scheduled belly-rub sessions.

No active treats detected. Routine patrols only.
LEVEL 1 – VISIBLE SNACK ACTIVITY
Open snack packages observed in human custody. Increased vigilance recommended.
LEVEL 2 – UNATTENDED TREAT SCENARIO
Snacks temporarily left without direct supervision. High temptation, strict protocol required.

HQ REGULATIONS
INTERNAL PROTOCOLS
  • Regulation 01 – Corridor Conduct Uncontrolled zoomies are restricted to designated zoomie lanes. Collisions with humans carrying hot beverages are to be avoided at all costs.
  • Regulation 02 – Treat Handling Protocol No operative may self-approve treat access. All snacks require human authorization, even if clearly mislaid on the coffee table.
  • Regulation 03 – Vocalization Policy Barking is permitted for security-relevant events only: doorbells, suspicious noises and the sound of treat bags. Excessive commentary on passing leaves or imaginary threats is discouraged.
  • Regulation 04 – Couch Occupation Rights Shared couch usage must follow the “One Paw Width” rule. Gradual expansion into human territory is allowed if no objection is raised.
  • Regulation 05 – Cuddle Priority During low-alert phases, cuddle requests from trusted humans have priority status. Operations may be temporarily paused for belly-rub objectives.

HQ-REGULARIEN
INTERNES PROTOKOLL
  • Regel 01 – Flurverhalten Unkontrollierte Zoomies sind auf ausgewiesene Zoomie-Spuren beschränkt. Kollisionen mit Menschen, die heiße Getränke tragen, sind unter allen Umständen zu vermeiden.
  • Regel 02 – Treat-Handling-Protokoll Kein Operative darf Treat-Zugriff eigenständig freigeben. Alle Snacks erfordern eine menschliche Autorisierung – selbst wenn sie offensichtlich unbeaufsichtigt auf dem Couchtisch liegen.
  • Regel 03 – Vokalisierungsrichtlinie Bellen ist nur bei sicherheitsrelevanten Ereignissen zugelassen: Türklingeln, verdächtige Geräusche und das Rascheln von Treat-Tüten. Übermäßiger Kommentar zu vorbeifliegenden Blättern oder imaginären Bedrohungen ist unerwünscht.
  • Regel 04 – Sofa-Belegungsrecht Gemeinsame Sofanutzung erfolgt nach der „Eine-Pfote-Abstand“-Regel. Eine schrittweise Expansion in den menschlichen Bereich ist zulässig, sofern kein Widerspruch erfolgt.
  • Regel 05 – Cuddle-Priorität In Phasen niedriger Alarmstufe haben Kuschelanfragen vertrauenswürdiger Menschen Priorität. Laufende Operationen dürfen zugunsten von Bauchrubbel-Zielen kurzfristig unterbrochen werden.
