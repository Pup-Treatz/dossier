K9 Special Unit — Est. 2025

AGENCY

Agency History & Operative Mandate

Wraff Force is a specialized K9 tactical unit operating wherever the global snack supply is at risk. Established in March 2025 following an incident in Berlin that remains only partially classified. We do not discuss the details. The pretzels were unrecovered. We moved on.

WF-AB-01

FOUNDING

On the afternoon of March 14, 2025, a pretzel stand in central Berlin was left unguarded for a duration of 4 minutes and 17 seconds. When supervision resumed, three pretzels, one bag of peanuts and one chocolate bar were missing. A single unidentified pawprint was found in the salt shaker. No witnesses came forward. The case was closed as unexplained snack loss.

Puppy Treatz, primary operative and founding member, was present at the scene. The incident was filed. The implications were noted. Three weeks later, Wraff Force was established. The pretzel stand no longer exists. This is officially attributed to renovation. No further questions are accepted on this matter.

Operative Mandate — WF-MANDATE-001
To secure, protect and where necessary recover the global snack supply. No biscuit left behind. No kibble left to fate. Wraff Force answers every call.

WF-AB-03

OPERATIVE DOCTRINE

Wraff Force operates under a strict set of tactical and behavioral principles. These are not suggestions. They are doctrine. Several sections of the Field Manual are still being finalized. This does not affect operational readiness.

01
THE SNACK IS SACRED
All snacks under Wraff Force jurisdiction are to be treated as mission-critical assets. No operative may consume a secured snack without explicit handler authorization. Consumption without authorization is logged. It is always logged.
02
73% IS SUCCESS
A snack recovery rate of 73% is the established benchmark for mission success. This figure was determined organically during Operation Crumb Recovery and has never been revised. It will not be revised.
03
ALL RANKS ARE RESPECTED
Colonel Flops holds the rank of Colonel. This rank is recognized. His counsel is sought. His silence is interpreted. He has never confirmed or denied any interpretation. This has not stopped anyone from asking.
04
INCIDENTS ARE FILED
All incidents — regardless of scale, cause or level of embarrassment — are to be documented and filed within 24 hours. 23 reports filed to date. 17 involve Unit Two. All explanations were technically plausible.
05
WE DO NOT DISCUSS JUNE 2025
This is not a guideline. This is doctrine. The holographic treat incident is classified under WF-TREAT-06. The file is sealed. There is nothing further.
06
CUDDLES ARE CONTRACTUAL
Unit Five has established that cuddles with Colonel Flops constitute a legitimate operational activity. Command reviewed this assertion. Command filed it. The duration is recorded. Everyone has moved on.
WF-AB-04

CORE VALUES

I
LOYALTY
Unwavering commitment to the unit, the handler and the mission. In that order. Unless treats are present. Then the order may vary. This is acknowledged in the Field Manual.
II
PRECISION
Every movement is deliberate. Every sniff is intentional. Every report is filed accurately. Except Appendix B. Appendix B is redacted. This will not change.
III
INTEGRITY
Wraff Force operates with full transparency — within classification guidelines. What can be disclosed, is disclosed. What cannot, is redacted. The redactions are always necessary.
IV
RESILIENCE
Wraff Force has endured squeaky toys, holographic treats, foil wrapper incidents and the ongoing Habinghorst Kitchen Situation. We are still here. We are still filing reports. We will not stop.
V
SNACK SECURITY
The protection of snack reserves is not merely a mission objective. It is a calling. No operative joined Wraff Force by accident. The snacks called to them. They answered.
VI
DOCUMENTATION
If it happened, it is filed. If it is filed, it exists. If it exists, it is subject to review. Unit Two is aware of this. This has not changed Unit Two's behavior in any measurable way.
